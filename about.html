A private, permanent reading list

Track what you read. Keep it forever. Your data is encrypted and yours alone — and Bookish is free to use.

Try Bookish

Private

Your reading list is encrypted before it leaves your device. You control who has access — and unless you share it, that's no one but you.

Permanent

Stored forever on a permanent network — not on servers that can shut down, get acquired, or lose your data.

Free to use

Save as many books as you like, free. No ads, no surprise tiers, no selling your reading data. Optional paid plans may arrive later to support permanent storage.

How it works

1

Add your books

Search by title or type them in. Add cover art, reading dates, notes — whatever matters to you.

2

Create an account

Sign up with your email and a password. Free storage included to get you started — no credit card needed.

3

Access it anywhere

Sign in on any device and your books are there. Encrypted, synced, and permanently saved.
